About Us

This project was designed and developed by Griffin Tschurwald, Greg Coleman, Maor Bernstein, Kevin Suzuki, and Sawyer Vaughan. We're a team of students studying to be engineers at the Franklin W. Olin College of Engineering in Massachusetts. This app was our final project for our Software Design class.

About the Model

Our program was designed to build a model for dependence of stock price upon volume, and competitor's stock prices and volumes. To build the model, we use multiple regression to determine the price's dependence. Obviously, the stock market is very complex, and if anybody could easily predict future trends, the stock market would be a broken system. This is the reason for our prediction being very short term. We've done our best, but obviously if you're actually buying stocks, you should seek professional advice.
